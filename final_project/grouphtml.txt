<p>Overall employment in architecture and engineering occupations is projected to grow faster than the average for all occupations from 2024 to 2034. About 186,500 openings are projected each year, on average, in these occupations due to employment growth and the need to replace workers who leave the occupations permanently.</p>
<p>The median annual wage for this group was $97,310 in May 2024, which was higher than the median annual wage for all occupations of $49,500.</p>
